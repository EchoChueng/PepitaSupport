Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I save my notes?
A: Your notes are automatically saved locally on your device as you click done on edit page.

Q: Can I backup my notes?
A: Currently, all notes are stored locally on your device. We recommend regularly backing up your device to preserve your data.

Q: Who can see my notes?
A: Your notes are private and stored only on your device. No one else has access to them.
